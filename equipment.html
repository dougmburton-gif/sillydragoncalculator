🤯 Genuine "Wow" Size Facts

⛏️

Bagger 293 is a bucket-wheel excavator — one of the largest land vehicles ever built. It's about 96 meters (315 ft) long, 225 meters (738 ft) tall, and weighs roughly 14,200 tons.

Widely documented, including by Guinness World Records and TAKRAF (its manufacturer).
🚜

Big Bud 747 is considered the largest farm tractor ever built — 900 horsepower, weighing about 95,000 lb, with tires taller than an adult.

Documented by the Big Bud tractor museum and multiple agricultural history sources.
🚛

Liebherr T 282C is one of the largest dump trucks in the world — about 14.5 meters (48 ft) long, and can haul roughly 363 tons in a single load.

Published by Liebherr's own equipment specifications.
