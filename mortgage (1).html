Mortgage Calculator

Enter your loan details — the green dragon inhales each digit and breathes out your monthly payment.

This free online mortgage calculator estimates your monthly principal and interest payment based on loan amount, interest rate, and loan term. Useful for first-time homebuyers comparing loan scenarios or estimating affordability before speaking with a lender.

How this is calculated

Uses the standard fixed-rate amortization formula for principal & interest only. This is an estimate for planning purposes, not a loan offer — property tax and insurance aren't included here.
