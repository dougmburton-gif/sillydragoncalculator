📚 Free Homeschool Curriculum

A short list of genuinely free homeschool resources — complete scope-and-sequence programs, free digital textbooks, and strong free subject-specific courses. Nothing here requires a credit card.

🎓 Built around learning practices commonly promoted by leading university teaching centers.
⚠️ When choosing any educational curriculum for a minor, you must first consult with your local and state board of education. Homeschool requirements (registration, testing, subject requirements) vary by state and country — this list is a starting point for research, not legal or educational guidance.
Complete Curriculum · K–12

Easy Peasy All-in-One Homeschool

A complete, ready-to-use curriculum covering math, reading, science, history, writing, and electives for grades K–12 (high school continues at allinonehighschool.com). Christian, eclectic/Charlotte-Mason style. 100% free, always — no paid tier.

Note: relies on external links (videos, reference pages) that occasionally break over time.
Classical · Pre-K–12

AmblesideOnline

A free Charlotte Mason–style classical curriculum spanning pre-K through Year 12 (roughly ages 4–18), strong in literature, history, geography, nature study, and art/composer study. No tuition, dues, or required donations — you only pay for physical books if you don't use the many free public-domain texts available online.

Core Subjects · Ages 2–18

Khan Academy

Free courses across math (K through calculus), science, grammar, economics, history, and computer programming, plus SAT/AP prep. Khan Academy Kids (a separate free app) covers ages 2–8. Nonprofit-funded — genuinely 100% free, no premium unlock.

Math & Science · K–12

CK-12 Foundation

Free, customizable digital textbooks ("FlexBooks"), simulations, and adaptive practice, concentrated in math and science for grades K–12 (with some college-level material too). Fully free, nonprofit, no paywall.

Knowledge-Sequenced · K–8

Core Knowledge Foundation

A content-rich, knowledge-sequenced curriculum (Language Arts, History & Geography, Science) for grades K–8, built on their published Core Knowledge Sequence. The Sequence itself and many student readers/teacher guides are free downloads.

Note: some polished packaged programs or printed materials may carry a cost — check per item.
Language Arts Only · K–8

The Good and the Beautiful (Free Language Arts)

Free, downloadable Language Arts courses for grades K–8 — full PDF courses covering phonics, reading, writing, grammar, and spelling. Their math and other subjects are paid programs, so this is specifically a free Language Arts pick, not a full free catalog.
