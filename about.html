🐲 About Silly Dragon Calculator

Who We Are

Silly Dragon Calculator is a small, independently-run website. It's built and maintained by a single site owner, not a company or agency — the goal from day one has been to make something genuinely useful and a little bit fun, not just another generic calculator page.

What We Offer

The site started as a set of free browser-based calculators (basic, scientific, graphing, and mortgage) with a playful dragon theme. It's since grown to include word games, a hobby farm and dog breed directory, a free homeschool curriculum guide, and a few "just for fun" extras. Everything runs directly in your browser — nothing you type into a calculator is ever sent to a server or stored.

How the Site Stays Free

Silly Dragon Calculator is supported by advertising and by affiliate links to products related to the topic at hand (for example, on the Hobby Farm and Dog Breeds pages). If you click one of those links and make a purchase, the site may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
