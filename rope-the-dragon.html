Ready to Rope a Dragon?

Click the dragon to catch it. Every 5 catches levels you up — it gets faster, and at level 3+ it might breathe fire back!

How to play

Click or tap anywhere near the flying dragon to throw your rope. A catch earns a point and the dragon resets somewhere new, a little faster than before. Every 5 catches takes you up a level. From level 3 onward, the dragon can turn around and breathe fire at you — if it catches you, that's game over (a happy one — nobody actually gets hurt around here). Your best score is saved in this browser.
